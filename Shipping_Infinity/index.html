About Us

BIGSHIPMENT GLOBAL LIMITED is located in Houston, TX and have associates all over the world. We have a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and provide uncompromising quality and efficiency in all aspects of maritime shipping. We have made strategic investments to establish main trunk routes, dedicated terminals and a strong infrastructure has been the bedrock of our phenomenal growth despite our 20 year short history. As we continue to grow, our foremost target is to strengthen our capability to provide the highest performance level with cost-effective measures in order to meet growing demands of customers. This will help us to achieve our goal of becoming the "world's most trusted carrier".
